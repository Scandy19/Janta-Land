Sanawar Hills, Himachal Pradesh

Property Description

Sanawar Hills is JLPL’s (Janta Land Promoters Pvt Ltd) prestigious landmark project at Kasauli. It consists of 39 Exclusive Free-hold Independent Luxury Villas ranging from 2860 Sq.Ft to 6807 Sq.Ft. (approx) along with Servant Accommodation. This Residential abode is in the exquisite environment of nature amidst thrilling atmosphere of hills.

This project will let the nature pamper you with her absolute beauty and serenity. Extra amenities in the form of an exclusive Club House enchantingly supported by the comfort, leisure & nostalgic luxury of green surroundings makes it like a Heavenly Retreat.

Overview

What if you wake up everyday to a good morning kiss pecked on your cheek by a warmth carrying streak of nature! What if the chirping birds and fresh twirls of trees welcome you every morning as you move out of your house for work! With Janta Land Promoters Pvt. Ltd's project at Kasauli, this dream seen by many can surely become a reality. This project aims at making nature keep pampering you.

Kasauli, nestled at a height of 5600 feet( 1706 mts. ), is a little town overlooking Himalayas and Dhauladars. Walking through the pine trees while feeling its colonial ambience is a common thing in this town. It is one of the most sought after tourist destinations in Himachal Pradesh. With Shimla at a distance of 77 Km, Kasauli is just one and a half hours drive from Chandigarh. The famous "The Lawrence School", one of the best in the country, is at a walking distance from these Villa.

Salient features of this heavenly abode:

  • 60 kms from Chandigarh, 2 kms from reputed Lawrence School at Sanawar.
  • Independent detached villas.
  • Green verdant landscape with pedestrian walkways and natural pine vegetarian.
  • Approximately 80 % open and green spaces.
  • Wide roads and campus with ornamental street lights.
  • Fire prevention and fighting as per safety norms.
  • Energy efficient product. Rain water harvesting system.
  • Independent servant room with toilet in every villa.
  • 24 hours water supply.

Sky Gardens, Sector-66 A, Mohali

Overview

After successful completion of Mega Project of 393.50 acre the company is presently developing "Super Mega Mixed Use Integrated Industrial Park Project" with Sky Gardens being a major residential component of the project. The total area to be developed under this scheme is 263.38 acres which falls under Sector 66 – A, 82 & 83 Mohali. The project lies adjoining Mohali Railway Station and Chandigarh/Mohali International Airport. Sky Gardens is situated in Sector 66 – A, on the 100 ft. road which is connected to 200 ft wide International Airport road. The façade is in pleasing pure rectilinear form further enhanced by vertical louvers throughout the height of the building. It is truly a unique development that will shape the future.

Salient Features

  • Over 65000 sq. ft. of uninterrupted Green Ecodeck.
  • Approximate 5000 sq. ft. premium Club House with convenience store
  • 1 Passenger lift and 1 service lift per tower.
  • Gated and 24 hours manned perimeter security for the complex.
  • Intercom & Internet connection in each flat. Energy efficient product.
  • Fire prevention and fighting as per safety norms.
  • Exhaust fans provision in the Kitchen & Toilets. Separate utility area in kitchens.
